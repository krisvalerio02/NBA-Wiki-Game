About the NBA Wiki Game

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

This web app is a game/wiki for NBA facts and trivia!

Game consists of being presented a silhouette of a random NBA player

as well as hints regarding that player and you must guess who it is!

How to play

You can play by selecting the 'Play' tab and choosing which game mode you would like to play

Users are initially given one hint, they may request more but the less hints you request the more valuable the card you earn.

The cards you earn in the play mode are displayed in the 'collection' tab.
